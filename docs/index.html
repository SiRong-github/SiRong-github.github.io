Emotional Pizzeria

Emotional Pizzeria is a game designed to enhance emotional granularity in autistic children.

Inspired by Papa's Pizzeria, the game invites players to match an emotional pizza with the emotion described by a customer's personal experience. Players receive hearts based on the number of attempts it took for them to find the closest emotional fit.

Utilising agile methodology, we ensured the game evolved according to client feedback and needs. In our latest demo, the client expressed great satisfaction with the game, highlighting its effectiveness in achieving its educational goals.

Fly By

Fly-by is an endless runner game in which the player travels through the sky as a plane or a hot air balloon.

As they move forward, they will encounter different obstacles, weather conditions and power-ups.

The obstacles and weather conditions are designed to make their journey difficult. For example, the fog and night mode will induce poor visibility, making it easier for the player to hit the obstacles.

Conversely, the power-ups will help them along their journey. Depending on the power-up they obtain, they may either be granted a period of invincibility or both invincibility and a massive speed boost! The aim of the game is to fly as far as possible without crashing into an obstacle. If the player crashes into an obstacle, their jouney will come to an end!
